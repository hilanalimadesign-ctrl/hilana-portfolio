About

Hi! I’m Hilana, but you can call me Lana :)

I’m a Product Designer focused on turning complex ideas into simple, intuitive and meaningful digital experiences. I work across UX and UI, from research and user flows to high-fidelity interfaces, always balancing usability, visual clarity and accessibility.

I have experience designing B2B platforms, mobile apps, financial products, logistics systems and digital identity flows, collaborating closely with stakeholders and development teams to create scalable product experiences.

Beyond design, I’m also a digital illustrator and the founder of Caçadoras de Hobbies, a creative community based in Recife. In my free time, you’ll probably find me reading on my Kindle or exploring the world of code — this website was born from that curiosity 💻✨
