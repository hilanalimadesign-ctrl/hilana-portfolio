Redesign of a logistics platform for asset management, traceability and operational flow monitoring.

Overview

Retrack is a digital solution developed by iTrack to support companies managing large volumes of assets in circulation, providing greater traceability, automation and operational control throughout the logistics chain.

The project focused on redesigning a complex operational platform, making the experience clearer, more scalable and adaptable to different user profiles and client organizations.

I worked on the platform redesign by analyzing the existing product, understanding operational workflows, mapping user needs, designing interfaces, creating reusable components and adapting the solution to different business contexts.

01 • Context

A logistics operation with multiple stakeholders, stages and responsibilities

Managing asset flows involves different participants, such as control towers, carriers, dealers, customers and internal teams responsible for the operation.

Each user profile needed to access specific information, perform actions at different moments and track asset status throughout the journey.

The challenge

Structuring a highly complex operational system without making the experience overwhelming, confusing or dependent on constant support.

02 • Before the redesign

Identifying opportunities to improve the existing experience

The previous platform already supported important operational processes, but presented challenges related to visual organization, hierarchy, scanability and consistency across screens.

05 • Operational Flows

Organizing a journey with multiple statuses and scenarios

The operation needed to support several asset statuses, such as on time, arriving, pending adjustments, scheduled pickup, collected, in transit, delivered, inspection, completed, delayed, in stock, rejected and warranty.

The redesign organized these statuses into a consistent visual logic, allowing users to quickly understand the current stage of the operation and the available actions.

Design decision

I used colors, filters, tabs, tables and visual states to improve quick scanning and decision-making in complex operational scenarios.

06 • Client Customization

A flexible platform for different operations

Retrack was adapted for different client companies, including AGCO, Brenntag and AssetShift. Each operation had specific data structures, terminology, steps and responsibilities.

In Brenntag's case, for example, it was necessary to understand information coming from support spreadsheets, such as asset type, code, order, invoice, quantity, customer, return data and documents involved in the process.

Overview of the platform flow and its client-specific adaptations.

What this project strengthened in my design practice

Retrack reinforced my ability to work on complex B2B products, where the experience needs to balance clarity, operational efficiency, business rules and different responsibilities across users.
